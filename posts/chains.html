BREAKING THE RENTAL CHAINS

// LOG DATE: 2026-01-20

You do not own your software. You rent it. You pay a monthly tribute to Adobe, to Microsoft, to Netflix. If you stop paying, your tools vanish. Your work is held hostage.


This is feudalism. We are digital serfs tiling the land of tech giants.


Source Arcanum is a rejection of this model. We build "Buy Once" or "Free Forever" software. Our PDF Manager (Codex Forge) does not ask for a subscription to sign a document. It does what it says, locally, forever.
