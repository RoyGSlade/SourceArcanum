## Full Description

**1-2 Paragraphs:**
(What it does, who it's for, what makes it different)

*Codex Forge* (Pdf Manager) is a pragmatic tool designed to break the chains of subscription-based PDF software. It is not meant to be a revolutionary overhaul of the file format, but a sovereign utility that lets you simply *work*. Whether you need to sign a document, add a field, or manipulate a page, this tool handles it locally and privately.

Its standout feature is the integration of local AI for Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and Text-to-Speech (TTS). It can take dense research papers or scanned images and convert them into audio files overnight, allowing you to "read" complex documents while driving or commuting. It transforms static, locked data into accessible, portable knowledge.

## Feature List

**6-12 Bullet Features:**
- **Local PDF Editing:** Add fields, sign documents, and manipulate pages.
- **Offline OCR:** Convert scanned images and PDFs into searchable, editable text.
- **Text-to-Speech (TTS) Conversion:** Turn entire documents into downloadable audio files.
- **Subscription-Free:** A one-time tool to replace costly monthly services.
- **Batch Processing:** "Chug away" at large documents overnight.
- **Image-to-Text:** Extract text capabilities for standard image files.
- **Dream Tool Integration:** Designed to work alongside the Dream Tool ecosystem.
- **Privacy First:** No cloud uploads; your documents stay on your machine.

**3 "Flagship" Features:**
1.  **Sovereign Signing:** Complete digital signatures and form filling without relying on third-party verification servers or unnecessary clouds.
2.  **Document-to-Audio:** A "read-it-later" engine that converts massive PDFs into audiobooks for passive consumption.
3.  **Universal OCR:** Unlocks text from any static image or PDF, making every document in your library searchable and accessible.
