REAL NAME: Infinite Ages Genesis

Explore infinite worlds across infinite ages.

// The architecture of the multiverse laid bare, granting you the power to weave destiny across stars and centuries without the chains of class or era.

*Infinite Ages Genesis* is a world simulation and narrative storytelling engine that breaks the mold of traditional TTRPGs. It is a completely classless system designed for player-driven and GM-driven storytelling, set within the vast "USSI" framework. This setting allows Game Masters to drop players into literally *any* world they can imagine—whether it's a grit-filled medieval planet, a sleek modern metropolis, a colonial frontier, or a chaotic sci-fi pirate flotilla—all while keeping the core mechanics consistent.

The system distinguishes itself with a unique "learning by doing" progression. Instead of arbitrary experience points, your character evolves through action. Utilize your "Artist" or "Athlete" skills, or engage in charisma-based diplomacy; success grants skill levels, which in turn drive your overall character level, PowerPoints, and HP. With 10 distinct superpowers featuring massive skill trees, a deep cybernetics system, comprehensive crafting, and a unique combat flow using a full 7-dice set (d20-d100), it offers a depth of play that is expansive yet manageable.

The ultimate horizon for *Infinite Ages* is the development of a proprietary Virtual Tabletop (VTT). Unlike commercial VTTs that lock data in the cloud, this will be a privacy-focused, self-hosted solution. It will allow GMs to host their own sessions directly from their machines (Direct Connect), ensuring that your campaigns, your worlds, and your stories remain yours alone. It represents the shift from just a book to a complete digital ecosystem for offline-first play.
