Deep Stone

REAL NAME: Deep Stone

Automated 2D top-down map creator for worlds.

// Prompt worlds to play in—a forge for imagining at a higher level and manifesting your dreamscapes into playable realities.

Deep Stone is an automated creator for 2D top-down maps, designed to let you "think out" your worldbuilding ideas. It goes beyond simple tile placement by offering a massive layered system—allowing for coherent structures with basements, floors, and subfloors. It serves as a rapid creation engine for TTRPG masters and game developers alike, giving them the tools to adjust, expand, and fine-tune the "vibe" of their world without painstakingly placing every pixel.

It allows you to import custom tile sets or works from artists and "transcribe" them into workable building blocks. The system learns from your critiques, refining its generation to match your personal style. It acts as a bridge between imagination and table-ready assets, and will eventually power the generation of environments for the *Infinite Ages* system.
