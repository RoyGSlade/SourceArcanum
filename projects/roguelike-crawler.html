Iron Descent

REAL NAME: Roguelike Crawler

A turn-based LLM-driven dungeon crawl demo.

// A descent into a never-ending dark, where the world remembers your bones even as your new soul takes its first breath.

*Iron Descent* is a "play pretend" engine—a piece of interactive entertainment designed to demonstrate the capabilities of the *Deep Stone* map creator and the *Infinite Ages* ruleset. It is a 2D top-down, turn-based roguelike where the dungeon master is a simple but elegant local LLM. It allows users to run through solo adventures with randomized loot and NPCs, experiencing the mechanics of the larger system in a contained, fun environment.

Currently, this project sits low on the priority list, serving as a reward for when the productivity tools are complete. It creates a space for pure enjoyment, testing the limits of what a browser-based, offline-first AI game can look like.

CAPABILITIES

// SPECS

Local LLM Dungeon Master:

An AI that narrates and manages the adventure offline.

Infinite Ages Ruleset:

Runs on the same mechanics as the TTRPG.

Deep Stone Map Integration:

Uses maps generated by the *Deep Stone* tool.

Persistent World State:

The world remains and remembers previous runs/deaths.

Turn-Based Combat:

Classic tactical gameplay.

Randomized Loot & NPCs:

Endless variety in every run.

Static Page Architecture:

Runs entirely in the browser without complex backend requirements.
