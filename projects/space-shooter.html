Stardust

REAL NAME: Stardust

Physics-based space racing and crystal collection demo.

// Dance through the void on the edge of drift, harvesting the crystallized light of dead stars before the darkness catches up.

*Stardust* (formerly "Roadmap Game") is a study in momentum and mastery. It is currently a tech demo focused on high-speed traversal using real physics. Players must navigate asteroid fields, collect crystals, and manage their ship's drift to achieve the fastest times.

While currently a solo experience, it serves as the foundation for a much larger vision: a chaotic, tag-team tactical shooter where piloting and systems management are split between two players. For now, it remains a testbed for flight mechanics and a challenge for those who enjoy the purity of time trials.
